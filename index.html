About Me

Hi, my name is Raima and I've decided to start a tutoring service for kids in kindergarten through the seventh grade. I'm in eighth grade myself, and I find myself having a lot of free time, so I'd like to use that time to teach others. I teach over video calls, so I'll be able to share my notes with the student through screen sharing. At school, some subjects I like are math, language arts, social studies, and Spanish. Those are some of the subjects I tutor in. Some other things I like to do outside of school are reading, doing puzzles, painting, and writing.

 

How It Works

Subjects

Materials

I tutor in Math, Spanish, Language Arts, Science, Social Studies, and Basic Python. For math, I can tutor from Kindergarten Math to Algebra 1. For Spanish, I can tutor up to Spanish 1 and can teach eighth-graders.

The materials a student will need in a lesson are usually just a notebook or a note-taking device, something to write with, and a device with a good internet connection.  

How I'll Teach

I do one on one lessons over an app called Zoom, a video call app. During these lessons, I'll be taking notes on the subject on an app called Notability, which I will screen share on the call, so the student can understand what I'm teaching better.
